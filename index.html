Rabi Oscillations Simulation

How does the spin of an electron-like particle with:
  • mass m = electron mass
  • charge q = e
  • initial state (a + bi, c + di) = ( + i , + i )
oscillate in a magnetic field with B = Gauss, B0 = Gauss, and omega = rad/s ?
Made for the McGill Physics Hackathon 2020 by Christian Hellum-Bye, Leo Goutte, Cheng Lin, and Su Goh.
